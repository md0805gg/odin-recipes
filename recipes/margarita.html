"White Christmas" Margarita

Description

Sweet coconut cream, fresh lime juice, and tequila combine for a drink that's as tasty as it is fun! Serve it up at your next holiday party and it's sure to become a tradition!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 oz. agave, plus more for glass rim
  • Angel flake coconut
  • 2 oz. tequila
  • 1 oz. fresh lime juice
  • 1 oz. unsweetened coconut cream

Steps

  1. Place agave on a small plate. Place coconut on a second small plate. Dip the rim of a rocks glass in the agave then in the coconut.
  2. Combine agave, tequila, lime juice, coconut cream, and ice in a cocktail shaker. Shake until very cold, about 1 minute. Pour into prepared glass. Serve immediately.
